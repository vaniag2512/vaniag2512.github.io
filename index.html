Water Pollution in America

Reduce its existence. Increase its awareness

The Message

NRDC Source: https://www.nrdc.org/stories/water-pollution-everything-you-need-know#whatis

Many Americans live without knowing the severity or frequency of water pollution in the United States. Undetected chemicals are plaguing our water system. Large industries can pollute water by dumping chemicals into the ground or the sewer. Even with water treatment facilities, these chemicals can reach us and cause harm.
